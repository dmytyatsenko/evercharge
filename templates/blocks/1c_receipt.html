CTEP Certified Energy Metering

CTEP certification is required for all charging stations(EVSE) that handle billing of any kind.* This certification requires charging stations(EVSE) to meet the high levels of energy metering accuracy to ensure transparency throughout the billing process. EverCharge believes that transparency is key to a great end-user experience.

What it comes down to is the drivers are able to accurately reimburse the buildings they are drawing power from.

  • First EVSE to receive CTEP certification
  • *CTEP certification required in California as of January 1, 2021 for all new charging stations accepting payment
