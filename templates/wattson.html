About Wattson

Wattson is a fully enclosed power station with SmartPower technology built-in. In addition to acting as a stand-alone Level 1 charging station in its own right, Wattson can also support high-powered Level 2 charging.

This allows Wattson to rapidly charge and power an array of products from electric cars, golf-carts, e-bikes, electric scooters, and even offer trickle charging to high-end luxury vehicles.
