How it Works
Our patented power management technology not only allows us to scale up to 10x more than other solutions, it also means we get commercial electricity rates, which saves everyone money.


Our charging system connects to readily available common-area power in the building.


Each EverCharge unit in the building uses the same power source, with the power balanced between each unit.



Each unit has a meter that keeps track of the electricity consumed.


Every month we bill customers for the electricty consumed, and reimburse the building for the electricity all EverCharge units consumed.

Our Patented Power Management System

EverCharge learns each driver's unique behavior through our Power Management System. The system splits available power and allocates it to vehicles as needed - ensuring every EverCharge customer gets the power they need, when they need it.

This patented technology increases the charging capacity of multi-tenant buildings by up to 10 times. Meaning other charging systems would only allow one to charge at a time, EverCharge leverages existing power to allow mulitple cars to charge at once. All without expensive electrical upgrades.
