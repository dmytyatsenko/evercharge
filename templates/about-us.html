We are EverCharge

EverCharge provides the only EV charging system designed specifically for condos, apartment buildings, and parking garages. Our charging units install directly into a resident’s parking space for exclusive use.

What makes us different? We offer the only fully managed and affordable solution for EV charging. We’ve made things easy by providing insurance, maintenance, support and billing services for every customer.

Plus, our Power Management Technology enables you to maximize your building’s charging potential by up to 10x, avoiding costly infrastructure upgrades and lowering installation costs.

How it Works

Our system connects to readily available common area power in the building. This enables us to get commercial electricity rates and to easily scale throughout the garage for additional users.

Each unit meters the electricity consumed. We then bill the resident and fully reimburse the HOA.

All members receive ID Cards that your charging unit recognizes - just tap your card and charge away.




{% endblock %} {% block content_three %}
Power Management System

EverCharge uniquely learns driver behaviors through its Power Management System. The system splits the available power and allocates it to vehicles as needed.

This increases the charging capacity by up to 10x, without the need for expensive electrical upgrades.
