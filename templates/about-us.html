Our Patented Power Management System

EverCharge learns each driver's unique behavior through our Power Management System. The system splits available power and allocates it to vehicles as needed - ensuring every EverCharge customer gets the power they need, when they need it.

Overview

EverCharge’s power management technology maximizes a building’s potential by significantly increasing the number of EVs that can charge at a given site before costly infrastructure upgrades are needed. Power is intelligently managed based on the number of vehicles using the system and available power for charging. When EverCharge senses extra power is available, or that the rated capacity is reached, the system allocates power to vehicles according to their needs.

Power management maximizes total charging while preventing system overloads. In short, power management will maximize your building's infrastructure usage while reducing costs.

How the Technology works

EverCharge within the same garage, communicate with one another over a wireless mesh network. This network enables the system to perform real-time load balancing to ensure the system remains under the rated capacity at all times.

As a user plugs in his or her car and taps their RFID card, the system performs a series of safety checks confirming the available power among the charge group. In the event multiple chargers among a charge group are plugged which will exceed the power allocated to a charge group, the system will then redistribute the available power among vehicles.
