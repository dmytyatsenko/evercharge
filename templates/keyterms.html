EVERCHARGE MEMBER KEY TERMS

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and promises contained herein, the Parties hereby agree as follows:

1.1 Service Provider and Member Obligations
1.1.1 Service Provider Obligations
The Service Provider shall:
A. Provide billing services to Member for the electrical use of a Charging Station, pursuant to protocols as outlined in additional documentation provided to Member;
B. Enable line load management for Charging Station at the Development by utilizing a proprietary technology;
C. Recommend and connect the Member with a qualified and licensed electrical contractor for code-compliant installation, permitting, and approved inspection of the Charging Station;
D. Seek necessary approval for the installation of a Charging Station from any required additional persons or organizations, such as property managers, homeowners associations, building owners, etc.
E. Provide Charging Station authentication hardware including swipe or proximity cards and, if needed, issue replacements;
F. Provide Charging Station usage logging information upon request, including use by total time;
G. Enroll Member in a $1,000,000 liability umbrella insurance policy and provide terms of coverage, insurer information, claim management plan, and acknowledgment of enrollment;
H. Only assign any right or interest under this Agreement with the advance written consent of the Member, which consent may be withheld at the Member’s sole discretion. However, in the event of an acquisition by another entity, the Service Provider is entitled to assign rights and liabilities to the acquiring entity under this Agreement without advance written consent of the Member;
I. Any other services and actions necessary or appropriate to comply with the intents and purposes of this Agreement;
1.1.2 Member Obligations
The Member shall:
A. Provide Service Provider with accurate information regarding the vehicle make, model, year, and options and timely communicate any changes to this information;
B. Pay the initial cost of the Charging System to the Service Provider;
C. Allow Service Provider to utilize any part of a Member's Charging Station for the installation and operation of additional Charging Stations for existing or future EverCharge Members;
D. Allow Service Provider to implement auto-debit payment services or bill the Member for amounts due;
E. Seek resolution directly with Service Provider and not with the homeowners association, facility, property, or building management in the event of any dispute;
F. Authorize Service Provider to monitor the Member's Charging Station and electrical usage for any and all purposes, including for billing, to improve services or hardware, and for anonymous purposes of advertisement and promotion;
G. Provide access to the vehicle’s network communications control, if any, for purposes of facilitating the Member’s desired charging schedule in order to optimize charge sequencing;
H. Acknowledge that Service Provider is in no way responsible for any damage of any kind due to the use of any certified or code-compliant equipment provided directly or indirectly by Service Provider;
I. Acknowledge that Service Provider is unable to guarantee that the Member’s electric vehicle will always receive 100% complete electric charging in accordance with the Member’s charging preference despite that Service Provider is committed to apply best practicable charging schedule engineering to enable 100% electrical charging of the Member's electric vehicle in accordance with the Member’s charging preference.
J. Upon reasonable and appropriate notice of not less than 24 hours, grant the Service Provider and its employees, contractors, and representatives permission to enter the Development for purposes reasonably related to its duties hereunder.
K. Upon request and in a timely manner furnish or share data, information, and third-party information necessary and appropriate for the Service Provider to perform its duties hereunder, to the extent such information is reasonably available;
L. Not tamper with, damage, service, modify, or adjust the Charging Station, except to the extent necessary to properly protect and preserve the common area components of the Development or to address an emergency situation posing an imminent threat of injury or damage to persons or property within the Development;
M. Not assign the rights or liabilities under this agreement to another party without advance written consent from the Service Provider;
1.2 Delivery of the Services and Term
The Service Provider shall commence the provision of the Services on the date that the first Charging System is installed and verified as operational and in working order. The initial term of this Agreement shall be one (1) year from the Effective Date, with annual automatic renewal thereafter unless otherwise notified in writing from either party within 45 days of the scheduled renewal date.
1.3 Site and Placement
The Service Provider shall provide the Services at the Development, in an area designated by the Member and agreed upon by the Service Provider and the Member.
1.4 Price and Payment
A. Any charges due and owing for Services provided by the Service Provider to any Member (whether Services or otherwise), including, without limitation, any power consumption or monthly membership fees, shall be due directly to the Service Provider.
B. The Member is to be financially responsible for all costs incurred for the installation of an original Charging Station, a replacement Charging Station due to loss or theft, repair to a damaged Charging Station, or issuance of Authentication Hardware, whether original or replacement, unless otherwise indicated in this agreement.
C. All charges for the Services are exclusive of any applicable taxes, tariff surcharges, or other like amounts assessed by any governmental entity arising as a result of the provision of the Services, and such amounts shall be made payable to the Service Provider in addition to all other charges made payable hereunder.
D. In an effort to support new hardware, improve power management capability, maintain insurance and remain consistent with changing utility rate schedules, Service Provider reserves the right to modify pricing and/or reimbursement amounts or structure at any time. Any changes in pricing will be done to target similar monthly costs for the Member.
E. Service Provider may suspend Services if the Member is more than thirty (30) days late with any monthly payment and collect a late fee in order to reactivate Services.
