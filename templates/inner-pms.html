Benefits of EV Charging

EV Charging is a valuable service for you and your residents.


Increase property values. Providing EV charging for residents increases property values - and when you provide dedicated charging stations that residents can access at all times, this increase is even higher.



Attract residents. As the EV market continues to grow, more and more potential residents will be looking for a charging solution at home. Attract these residents with EverCharge's state of the art and scalable charging solution.



Increase tenant satisfaction. Providing a highly desirable service like EV Charging increases tenant satisfaction, and encourages long-term tenancy.



Differentiate your property. Less than 5% of condos and apartments currently offer EV charging - be on the cutting-edge and make your property stand out.



Lessen your properties' environmental impact. EVs significantly reduce carbon emissions, and in-home charging lessens the barrier to entry for EV car ownership.
