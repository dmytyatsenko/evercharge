Featured Properties

Located nationwide



Portland, OR

The 15-story high-rise has three levels of parking that are supported by a 200 amp panel dedicated for EV charging.

Before EverCharge, they would have only supported six chargers. Now, the building has the capacity to charge twenty vehicles simultaneously.

Miami, FL

Designed by renowned firm Arquitectonica the building rests on a ten-acre enclave on the edge of Biscayne Bay waterfront.

Residents parking in the 9-level garage simply contact EverCharge to get set up. As the single point of contact for EV charging, the management receives reimbursements for the power used.

Without a centralized electrical room, monitoring electricity would be impossible for residents at this property without EverCharge.

San Francisco, CA

This collection of 191 loft-style condominiums combines the best of lofts and condos in a unique and sophisticated package.

Without EverCharge the building only had capacity to support 3 residents. With over 6 EV owners, SmartPower technology makes it possible for more than ten residents to charge simultaneously making charging possible for years to come.

Chicago, IL

Originally constructed in the 1910’s as a cold storage facility, the building has been adapted as 186 loft-style homes with indoor parking garages.

Charge stations are fully managed and insured by EverCharge to ensure reliable charging and satisfied residents.

Los Angeles, CA

This modern, progressive 115-unit apartment community in the heart of Palms. With 6 assigned stations installed, interested residents simply contact EverCharge directly to gain access.

As the single point of contact for EV charging, the management just sits back and collects reimbursements for the power residents use.

Edgewater, NJ

The building provides panoramic views of the Hudson River, the George Washington Bridge, and the Manhattan skyline.

Utilizing our Power Management Technology, the building is able to save on infrastructure costs while supplying power to all interested residents within the garage.
