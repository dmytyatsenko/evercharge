How it Works

IMAGE GOES HERE

EV's need at least 208v power to charge -- a standard outlet is only 110v. To get the 208v power they need to charge, EVs must pull power directly from a building's electrical room. Evercharge does just this, running 208v, 220v, or 240v power from the electrical room to residents' assigned parking spaces, for their exclusive use.
Evercharge's chargers monitor the electricity being used during each charge. This allows us to balance power amongst active chargers, appropriately bill each resident, and reimburse the HOA for the common-area power the chargers used.
