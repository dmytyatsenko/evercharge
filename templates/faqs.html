What are the benefits of EverCharge?

  • Exclusive use: the charging unit is installed right in your parking spot.
  • Fully managed service: insurance, support, maintenance and automated billing services...we take care of everything.
  • Power Management: EverCharge intelligently manages buildings power to maximize your infrastructure potential up to 10x.

What type of electric vehicles can charge with EverCharge?

All EVs and Plug-in-hybrids sold in North America. This includes vehicles like the Tesla Model S, Nissan Leaf, Chevrolet Volt and Prius Plug-in.

Where is the charger installed?

Directly in your parking space for exclusive use. Access is granted by personal RFID key card.

Who will install the system?

An insured and licensed electrician. We only use the most reliable and competitively priced electrical contractors.

Do the units have wireless communication?

Our network boxes communicate wirelessly in the garage - no strings attached. This enables the Power Management System to direct power and transmit local data for billing and analytics.
