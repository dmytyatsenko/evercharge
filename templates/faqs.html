Basics



Benefits of EverCharge

EverCharge is a unique EV charging service that makes charging in condos and apartments easy. Without it, HOAs, property managers, and residents have to navigate the complexities of EV charging regulations, power balancing, and limited voltage by themselves. EverCharge handles all of this, providing:
  • Fully managed service: insurance, support, maintenance and automated billing services are all included -- we take care of everything.
  • Power Management: EverCharge intelligently manages buildings power to maximize your infrastructure potential up to 10x. Read more about power management.

Compatible Electric Vehicles

All Electric Vehicles sold in North America are compatible with the EverCharge charging system. This includes, but is not limited to.
  • BMW i3
  • Chevrolet Volt
  • Fiat 500e
  • Ford Focus Electric
  • Honda Fit EV
  • Kia Soul EV
  • Nissan Leaf
  • Tesla (all models)
  • Volswagen e-Golf

How does EverCharge work?

Our system connects to readily available common area power in the building. This enables us to get commercial electricity rates and to easily scale throughout the garage for additional users.

Each unit meters the electricity consumed. We then bill each resident for the energy they personally used, and fully reimburse the HOA for all energy used by EverCharge units on the property.

All members receive ID Cards that your charging unit recognizes - just tap your card and charge away. Our propietary power management technology balances power automatically, so residents never need to worry about charging.

Installation



Installation Location

All EverCharge chargers are installed directly in the owner's personal parking space for their exclusive use. A building that has multiple EverCharge customers will all use the same power source, but are still able to charge directly from their own space.

Access is granted to each charger by a personal ID card, exclusive to each customer.

We have several customers who live in buildings with valet or unassigned parking, and in these cases we work directly with the building to ensure that every resident still has 24/7, exclusive access to a charger.

Saving money on installation

When we install chargers, it often require significant infrastructure updates, and the costs of these are unavoidable. However, if you are an EV owner and property manager, you can significantly decrease your per-unit cost by installing multiple chargers at the same time.

Billing



Cost of Service

EverCharge bills you only for the energy you have used, based on the rate of your power provider. We also charge a small monthly fee of $15 which covers 24/7 lifetime support and $1,000,000 in liability insurance.
